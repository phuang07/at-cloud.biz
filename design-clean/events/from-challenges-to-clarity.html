From Challenges to Clarity: How Communication Shapes Your Career Journey

Start time

6:00 pm
October 27, 2025

Finish time

7:30 pm
October 27, 2025

Unlock the power of communication in your career

Join Dean Westbrook, Senior Director at Hewlett Packard Enterprise and bold, results-driven IT leader with 20+ years of experience across Sales, Services, and Operations, for an inspiring session.

In this session, Dean will explore how effective communication can turn workplace challenges into opportunities for growth. Drawing from his own career journey—from early setbacks to leading high-performing teams—he will share:

  • Personal stories and lessons learned
  • Practical strategies for overcoming obstacles
  • Insights on breaking down communication barriers
  • Techniques for building trust and using clear, intentional dialogue to drive success

Whether you’re navigating career transitions, leading teams, or looking to enhance your influence at work, this session will provide actionable tools to communicate with clarity and confidence.
