What is EMBA Program?

E
Entrepreneurship & Life Circle

Explore startup case studies, entrepreneurial qualities, and human motivation. Learn to distinguish analysis from opinions, balance entrepreneurship with life’s adventure through faith, and understand how the internet transforms society. Develop and present your business idea.

M
Melek & Preneur Circle

Core: Born and bonding in God’s kingdom, Called To Work. Release your future potential and learn to STOP at your BEST. Discover your strength areas, serve through mission/ministry at the right pace personally, and serve the Lord in public fields.

B
Business & Career Advancement Circle

Master effective communication, career renewal and change strategies. Understand the effect of family of origin in relationships, handle work relationships successfully, build partnerships, and develop leadership and management skills.

A
Apostolic Leadership Circle

Distinguish leadership from management. Master effective communication, networking, and influence. Explore attributes of true leaders, discuss insights from leadership books, exalt Christ in worship to seek God’s guidance, and apply learnings through real-life case studies and projects.
