About Us

Celebrating and expanding God’s kingdom & victories in the marketplace

Our Foundation

V
Vision
To celebrate and expand God’s kingdom & victories in the marketplace through planting Marketplace Churches inside companies.
M
Mission
Call and equip Christian leaders to make an impact in the marketplace.
Enable and support marketplace fellowships in Corporations.
Lead and transform more professionals to the Lord.

Our Objectives

  • Plant Marketplace Churches inside companies
  • Setup the Marketplace Manger — an incubator facility to enable perspective Kingdom-minded entrepreneurs to receive advice and mentorship from experienced coaches

Our Philosophy

Jesus is the very best Christian Entrepreneur the world has ever seen, even though you’ve never sat in a chair made by the human hands of that local carpenter. We need to integrate faith and work, live the way the Bible tells us to live at work, and work to testify the glory of God. We are saved by faith, not by works, and we do good works because we are already saved.

Your Calling

We invite Jesus to be our career development counselor and follow the guideposts Jesus has placed in our life. Careers are all about a holistic lifestyle in which we honor God and enjoy the process. There is no better example for Christian leadership than our Lord Jesus Christ. It is He who called the first apostles and imparted to them the life foundations for the roles they were to exercise.

If the above resonates with you, and you share the same burden, then you will finally understand your calling through this ministry. Your life will make sense for the first time! If you have ever thought about being called to be a marketplace ministry leader — you may find that God already has plans to cultivate you in your career. If you are not yet called to be a marketplace ministry leader at this time, you may still find this ministry will be valuable and beneficial to the business aspects of your calling. Therefore, whatever your calling is, @Cloud EMBA will open your eyes to something new that God is doing in your life and the world around us.
