Peter Hsi
View Bio

Peter Hsi received BS degree from Chiao Tong University in Taiwan and MS and PhD degree in EE from Syracuse University. He started his career working for several technology and equipment manufacturing companies. In 1984, he moved to Silicon Valley and joined a startup AOT Corp., a manufacturer of Automatic Test Equipment (ATE) for mixed signal semiconductor as VP of Engineering. The company was later sold to Hewlett Packard in 1991. Peter stayed in HP for a few years and then joined another startup company – RAE Systems Inc. in 1993 as a co-founder. RAE systems design and manufacture wireless gas and radiation sensors for industrial safety market. He served in RAE Systems as VP of Engineering, CTO and Board of Director. In 2013, RAE was acquired by Honeywell International Inc. He is currently the Director of Advanced Development Engineering and senior fellow in Honeywell Analytics Business.

Owen Wu
View Bio

Dr. Wu is a serial entrepreneur and coach/advisor for startups and fast growing companies in the areas of innovation, product development, manufacturing, corporate management, governance, and board advisor. Previously he was a serial intrapreneur at Gould and Hughes Aircraft Company. As a result, he received many awards including EO sensor awards, patent awards and prestige DoD Hershel award. During last 20 years, Dr. Wu was the cofounder and/or CEO of four high tech companies including FSC, ACT, UST (acquired by P.E Groups) and GCS (went IPO). Dr. Wu is active in serving communities. He was the founding chairman of southern Ca Monte Jade Association and Global Monte Jade chairman. He is the president of DNT International and Venture Commercial Commerce as well as the executive director of EMBA@Cloud marketplace ministry in Silicon Valley and Venture Partner/CSO of CoinX Group. Dr. Wu graduated from National Tsing Hua University and received MS and Ph.D from University of Illinois. He did postdoctoral research at Argonne National Lab. and attended Stanford executive management institute.
