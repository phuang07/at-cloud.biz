Program Structure

How the Program Is Organized

Every element is designed for both learning and relationship.

👥

Monthly Circle Meetings

Group mentoring led by your circle's mentor team — a consistent rhythm of teaching, discussion, and peer learning from May to November.

🎤

Bi-monthly Seminars

Expert speakers and marketplace topics organized by @Cloud every two months, open to the broader community.

🤝

1:1 Mentoring

Establish a personal mentoring relationship with your circle mentor for individualized guidance on your career, life, and calling.

🌐

Extra-Curricular Activities

Team-building sessions and learning-sharing events throughout the term — building genuine friendships beyond the formal sessions.

🎓

Graduation

A celebration in November honoring the completed EMBA term — with keynotes, mentee reflections, and the full @Cloud community.

🏆

Goals for Every Mentee

Learn from mentors and peers. Establish meaningful 1:1 relationships. Leave with lasting connections and a clearer vision for your marketplace calling.
