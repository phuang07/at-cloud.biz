About the celebration

Join us for an inspiring evening of gratitude, reflection, and celebration as @Cloud Marketplace Ministry honors the 2025 EMBA Mentorship Circle graduates! Together, we will celebrate the growth, resilience, and leadership of our mentees and mentors who have journeyed together in faith and purpose throughout the year.

This special gathering highlights graduates’ reflections and achievements—stories of transformation and renewed calling that embody @Cloud’s heart to empower marketplace leaders to live out their faith with excellence and resilience.

As we look forward to 2026 @Cloud, we will also share new opportunities for mentorship, coaching, discipleship, and community building events designed to reach the next generation and strengthen servant leadership.

Expect raffles, prizes, and joyful surprises!
