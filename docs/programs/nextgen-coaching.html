Why This Program Exists

Young Professionals Today
Face a Different Kind of Crisis.

AI is disrupting careers at the entry level. But the deeper challenge many young professionals face is not just finding a job — it's knowing who they are and where they're headed.

💼

Job Market Disruption

New grad hiring dropped 50% in 2024. AI is replacing entry-level tasks faster than anyone expected. Career paths that used to be clear are now uncertain.

🧩

Identity & Purpose Gap

Feeling alone in what you're experiencing. Imposter syndrome. Unclear on how your faith connects to your career. Wondering if your work even matters.

🌀

Gen Z Workplace Challenges

Many were hired during the pandemic and never learned how to navigate in-person dynamics. High expectations around growth, flexibility, and working with their boss and peers.

Questions we hear from young professionals

Who am I, really, beyond my résumé?
How do I stay faithful and hopeful when doors keep closing?
How do I design a meaningful career during AI disruption?
How do I bridge my faith and my identity at work?
How do I navigate relationships with my manager and peers?
How do I handle career, marriage, and life priorities all at once?

@Cloud NextGen Coaching was built to answer these questions — through structured teaching, honest mentorship, and a community of peers walking the same road.

How It Works

A Program Built Around You

Structured group learning combined with personal mentorship — so you grow in community and in depth at the same time.

This is not a passive online course. The @Cloud NextGen Coaching Track is a relational, interactive program where you learn alongside peers, receive individualized guidance from an experienced mentor, and apply what you learn in your actual career.

Every session follows a rhythm of teaching, discussion, and practical application — with assignments that ground the learning in your real life. Small cohort size ensures every participant gets genuine attention.

Group Teachings

Assignments & Projects

Practical exercises that bridge each session's teaching to your actual career and life — so growth happens between sessions, not just during them.

In addition to group sessions, NextGen mentors are available for more in-depth discussions in 1-on-1 settings — personalized guidance tailored to your specific goals, challenges, and calling.

Office Hours (Group)

Optional regular office hours hosted with at least one mentor present — a perfect forum for Q&A and case studies based on real-world challenges that come up in your work and life.

🌐

Social & Networking Events

Connecting with fellow young professionals and building a sense of community is highly valued — we provide opportunities for both online and in-person casual gatherings throughout the program.

Is This For You?

Who Should Apply

The Coaching Track is designed for a specific kind of person. Here's how to know if that's you.

✓ You're a great fit if you…
  • Are a new grad or young professional asking deeper questions about your career and calling
  • Want to understand who you are beyond your résumé and job title
  • Are navigating the workplace and looking for guidance with your manager, peers, and career growth
  • Are a Christian wanting to integrate your faith with your daily work life
  • Feel alone in what you're experiencing — imposter syndrome, uncertainty, pressure
  • Are open to structured learning, honest feedback, and real accountability
  • Want to build lasting friendships with like-minded young professionals
✕ This may not be the right fit if you…
  • Are looking for a purely technical or skills-based program (see our Internship Track)
  • Can't commit to the full session schedule and assignments
  • Prefer fully self-paced, on-demand learning only
  • Are not open to a faith-integrated coaching approach
